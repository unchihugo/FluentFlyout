Full Media Control

Pop out media controls when you adjust your volume, play/pause or forward. Includes Repeat All, Repeat One, Shuffle, and a seek slider if your media player supports it.

"Up Next" and Lock Keys

Handy flyouts show what's next when a song ends or display the status of your lock keys at a glance when you press the corresponding key.

Deeply Customizable

Choose from multiple layout styles and positions, change themes, Mica or Acrylic backdrop; personalize and tweak settings to make FluentFlyout truly yours.

🎨

Native Windows Design

Seamlessly blends with Windows 11 using Fluent 2 components and Mica blur.

🌓

Light & Dark Mode

Automatically matches your system theme and color preferences.

✨

Smooth Animations

Enjoy fluid transitions and animations that feel native to Windows.

📍

Flexible Positioning

Choose where the flyout appears on your screen to suit your workflow.

🎧

Media & Volume

Listens to both volume and media inputs for comprehensive control.

🪶

Lightweight

Sits unobtrusively in your system tray without impacting performance.

Available in Multiple Languages

FluentFlyout is continuously being translated into other languages thanks to our amazing community on Weblate.

Free Forever 🌱

FluentFlyout is completely free and open source. Enjoy all features without any cost, now and forever.
